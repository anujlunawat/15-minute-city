### The "Aha!" Moment
Compare these numbers to our previous two reports:
* **Node-Based:** 1.3% of the city network is a True 15-Min City.
* **Area-Based:** 2.5% of the city land area is a True 15-Min City.
* **Population-Based:** **17.7% of the people live in a True 15-Min City.**

This proves that Pune's highly walkable areas (Score 6) are incredibly dense. Even though they only occupy 2.5% of the geographic footprint, they successfully house nearly a fifth of the population! This is the ultimate metric for urban planners because it measures human impact rather than empty asphalt.
